Cur8 Visual Tutorial

Step-by-step guide showing exactly where to click and what to do

📱 Screen 1: Sign In

When you open Cur8, you'll see the login screen with a beautiful background and the Cur8 logo at the top.

1
Find the Email field

It's the first input box after the logo, labeled "EMAIL"

Tap here: The email input box | Enter: reveshnee@gmail.com (your login email)
2
Find the Password field

Below the email field

Tap here: The password input box | Enter: Your password
3
Tap the "Enter my haven" button

Gold/sage colored button at the bottom of the form

Tap here: The button labeled "Enter my haven" to sign in

Pro tip: Your session stays active, so you won't need to sign in every time you visit.

🏠 Screen 2: Your Home Screen

Once signed in, you land on your personalized dashboard. This is where you see an overview of all your havens.

1
Look at the top left

You'll see the Cur8 logo (gold + red koi icons) and your app name

2
See your stats

Below the greeting ("Good morning, Reveshnee") are three key numbers:

  • 282 saved — total items across all havens
  • 0 today — how many items you saved today
  • 8 havens — your gardens/collections
3
Find the haven cards

Scroll down to see all 8 havens displayed as cards. Each has a unique name and description.

Tap any card to open that haven and explore its contents
💡 Navigation buttons: Top-right corner has Search, Calm mode, Guide, and Help buttons. Try them out!

🌿 Screen 3: Inside a Haven (3-Panel Layout)

When you open any haven, you see a powerful 3-panel interface. Let's break it down:

1
Left Panel: Items List

Shows thumbnails of all your videos, images, and documents in this haven.

What to do: Scroll to see more items | Tap any item to preview it in the center panel
2
Center Panel: Preview

Shows the full content of the item you selected. Videos play inline, PDFs show page-by-page, images display at full size.

What to do: Watch videos, read documents, or view images right here without leaving the app
3
Right Panel: Item Details

Shows metadata, links, and any notes you saved with this item.

What to do: Tap the Edit button to add or change your notes about this item
4
Top Toolbar

Important buttons for quick actions:

  • Brain Dump — Capture fleeting thoughts
  • Reflect — Ask AI about your saves
  • Focus Sounds — Play ambient sounds

📂 Screen 4: Folders & Sorting

Below the stats bar is the powerful folder and sort interface.

1
Folder Bar

Shows all folders in the current haven. Each folder displays how many items it contains.

Tap "FOLDERS" (left side) to see all folders | Tap "New folder" (+) to create a new organization folder
2
Type Stats Pills

Shows how many items of each type: Videos, Images, Sounds, Documents

Tap any pill to filter and show only that type of content
3
Sort Button

Controls how items are arranged. Click it to choose from:

  • Newest saved (default)
  • Oldest saved
  • A – Z (alphabetical)
  • Z – A (reverse alphabetical)
  • Recently opened
  • By type (videos, then images, then documents)
Tap the Sort button and select your preference. The button turns gold when a non-default sort is active.

💾 Screen 5: Saving Content

There are multiple ways to save. Let's start with the main Save button.

1
Find the Floating Save Button

On the home screen, look for a floating pill button at the bottom-center of the screen (or in the sidebar on desktop).

Tap it to open the save dialog
2
Paste Your Link

If you have a YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or article link copied, the app will prompt you to paste it.

Paste your link in the text field and the app will auto-fetch the title, thumbnail, and description
3
Add Your Notes (Optional)

Type your own thoughts or insights about this item

Why do this? Your notes help you remember why you saved it and what you thought about it
4
Choose Which Haven

Select which of your 8 gardens this item belongs in (Koi Pond, Sanctuary, Ember, etc.)

Tap the haven dropdown and select the most relevant one
5
Choose a Folder (Optional)

If you have folders created, assign this item to one for extra organization

6
Save It!

Tap the "Save" button (gold/accent color) to confirm

Done! Your item is now saved and appears in the chosen haven
📲 Alternative Ways to Save:
  • On mobile: Share from YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram → select Cur8 in the share menu
  • Upload files: Tap "Upload" in the save dialog to add PDFs, Word docs, images, or voice recordings

🧠 Screen 6: Brain Dump - Capture Thoughts

This is one of the most powerful features. Use it to capture fleeting ideas before they disappear.

1
Open Brain Dump

Look for the floating gold pill labeled "Brain Dump" on the home screen (bottom-left on mobile, sidebar on desktop)

Tap it to open the thought capture panel
2
Type Your Thought

Use the big text area to write anything on your mind. Rambling is okay — the app will help you clean it up.

Just start typing whatever's in your head
3
OR Speak It

Don't feel like typing? Tap the Speak button (microphone icon) and just talk. The app transcribes in real-time.

Tap Speak and start talking. Tap Stop when you're done.
4
Attach Files (Optional)

Tap Attach to add voice recordings, images, or saved items from your havens

5
Use AI to Tidy (Optional)

If your thought is rambling, tap Tidy and the AI will clean it up and extract action items

What it does: Organizes your messy thought into a clear summary + key points + action items
6
Save Your Thought

Tap Save thought (the gold button on the right) to save it. Choose where:

  • Locally — stays in your Cur8 app as a note
  • Email — sends to yourself for backup
  • WhatsApp — sends to your WhatsApp for quick access
  • mem.ai — sends to your external memory system
💡 Pro Tip: Brain Dump is NOT just for random thoughts. Use it for meeting notes, ideas, captured overheards, or anything you want to remember and think about later.

🤖 Screen 7: Ask AI - Chat About Your Saves

While browsing a haven, have a conversation with AI about the content you've saved.

1
Open a Haven

First, open any haven (like The Koi Pond or Sanctuary)

2
Find the Ask AI Button

In the top toolbar, look for the Ask AI button (speech bubble icon)

Tap it to open the AI chat panel on the right side
3
Ask a Question

Type any question about the items in this haven. Examples:

  • "Summarize the main ideas from these documents"
  • "What are the common themes?"
  • "Help me brainstorm how to apply this to my work"
The AI has read your documents, so it can give you specific, personalized answers
4
Get the Answer

The AI responds with thoughtful, contextual answers. You can:

  • Copy the answer to your clipboard
  • Save to Brain Dump to keep thinking about it
  • Save as Reflection to keep it as a standalone thought
5
Keep Chatting

Continue asking follow-up questions. The AI remembers context from earlier in the conversation.

Your conversation history is saved so you can come back to it later

🎵 Screen 8: Focus Sounds & Calm Mode

Cur8 has two modes designed for your nervous system.

1
Focus Sounds

Tap the Focus Sounds button in the top toolbar (speaker icon)

Choose from: Rain, Ocean, Forest, Stream, Binaural 18Hz (focus), Binaural 40Hz (clarity), Brown noise
2
Let It Play

Once you select a sound, it loops indefinitely. Work with it playing in the background.

Why this works: Your brain starts associating that sound with deep focus, so it becomes a ritual
3
Calm Mode

Tap the Calm button (top-right, lotus icon)

What it does: Disables animations, hides notifications, gives a distraction-free view
4
When to Use Calm Mode

Use it when:

  • You're feeling overwhelmed
  • You need a visual break
  • You're focusing and don't want animations
  • The movement is distracting you

🔍 Screen 9: Search & Discover

Can't find what you're looking for? Use search or let the app surprise you.

1
Search

Tap the search icon at the top of any screen

Type what you're looking for: Title, description, haven name, or content within documents
2
See Results

Search returns matching items from across all your havens

3
Surprise Me

On the home screen, tap the gold Surprise me button

What it does: Picks a random item from your saves and shows it to you. Great for rediscovering forgotten saves!

📲 Screen 10: Install on Your Phone

Cur8 is a PWA (Progressive Web App). You can install it on your phone home screen for quick access.

1
Open Cur8 in Mobile Browser

On your phone, go to haven.vercel.app/cur8 in Chrome, Safari, or your mobile browser

2
Look for Install Prompt

Your browser should show an "Install" or "Add to Home Screen" prompt

If you don't see it: Tap the menu (three dots) and select "Add to Home Screen" or "Install"
3
Tap Install

Follow the prompts to add Cur8 to your home screen

4
Done!

Cur8 now appears as an app on your home screen, and opens full-screen like a native app

Bonus: Updates happen automatically when you open the app

🎬 Tips for Recording Your Tutorial Video

When you record using Loom or similar tools:

  • Enable Calm Mode while recording so there are no distracting animations
  • Pause on each screen for 3-5 seconds so viewers can follow
  • Use a normal pace — don't rush. Viewers want to follow along
  • Test audio — record in a quiet space so your voice is clear
  • Record in fullscreen if possible for the clearest view
  • Do a practice take first — it helps you get comfortable with pacing

This guide covers the essentials. As you use Cur8, you'll discover more features and workflows that work for you. Enjoy!
