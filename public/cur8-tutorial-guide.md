# Cur8 Tutorial Guide

Welcome to Cur8 — your personal haven for curating, organizing, and discovering meaningful content. This guide walks you through every feature of the app.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Sign In
When you first open Cur8, you'll see a login screen. Enter your email and password to access your haven.

**What to do:**
- Enter your email address
- Enter your password
- Click "Enter my haven"
- You'll land on the home screen

---

## 2. The Home Screen (Your Haven)

The home screen is your dashboard. It shows you everything at a glance: your saved content, daily rituals, and quick access to your 8 gardens.

### Key Sections:

**Know Yourself**
- A section for AI-powered reflections and self-discovery
- Click the icon to open the AI Hub and explore topics, chat with AI about your saved content, and track past discussions

**The Pond (Your Daily Rituals)**
- Four interactive ritual orbs: Intention, Breathwork, Focus, and Calendar
- Tap any orb to open its widget and engage with that ritual
- The pond is your anchor — return here daily for grounding

**Recently Visited**
- Shows the 8 items you've opened most recently
- Quick way to get back to content you're actively using

**Your Havens (The 8 Gardens)**
- The Koi Pond: Lectures, modules & research reads
- The Current: Client research, saves & work tools
- The Greenhouse: Fashion, style & inspiration
- Sanctuary: Wellness, meditation & nervous system work
- The Grove: Psychology research, talks & books
- Ember: Inspiration, mood & things that spark joy
- Bloom: Music, shows & pure fun
- The Tide: Documents hub (SOPs, PDFs, everything else)

---

## 3. Saving Content

### Quick Save (Floating Pill)
A gold "+" button floats at the bottom of every screen.

**To save content:**
1. Tap the "+" button
2. Paste a link (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, articles, etc.) OR upload a file
3. Choose which haven to save it to
4. Add optional notes or attachments
5. Tap "Save"

### Brain Dump (Capture Thoughts)
When you have a fleeting idea or note, don't let it disappear.

**To brain dump:**
1. Tap the Brain Dump floating pill (bottom left)
2. Type or dictate your thought using the mic button
3. Optional: Attach files, voice notes, or use AI "Tidy" to organize your thoughts
4. Tap "Save thought"
5. Your thought is automatically sent to a designated email (you can customize where thoughts go)

### Reflections (Deep Thoughts)
For more structured self-reflection, use the Reflections tool.

**To reflect:**
1. Tap "Reflect" in the floating toolbar
2. Choose a reflection prompt or write your own question
3. Write or record your reflection
4. Attach items, voice notes, or images
5. Save — your reflection is tracked and can be reviewed later

---

## 4. Organizing in Havens

Once you've saved content to a haven, here's how to organize it.

### Folders
Create custom folders within each haven to group related items.

**To create a folder:**
1. Open any haven (e.g., The Koi Pond)
2. Tap the "New folder" button
3. Name your folder
4. Move items into it by selecting them

### Filtering & Sorting
See exactly what you're looking for.

**To filter:**
- Tap the type pills (Videos / Images / Documents / Sounds) to show only that content type
- Tap "FOLDERS" to filter by folder

**To sort:**
- Tap the "Sort" pill to choose: Newest saved, Oldest saved, A–Z, Z–A, Recently opened, or By type

### Selecting Multiple Items
Organize in bulk.

**To select multiple items:**
1. Tap "Select" in the toolbar
2. Tap items to check them
3. Use the batch bar at the bottom to: Move to folder, Copy to another haven, Share, Delete, or Download

---

## 5. Viewing & Interacting with Content

### Opening an Item
Tap any item (video thumbnail, image, document, or link) to view it.

### Preview Panel
The centre panel shows:
- Videos & images play inline
- PDFs and Word docs display with full-page viewing
- Articles show with the title and description

### Item Details
In the details bar, you can:
- **Edit** — Change the title or add/edit your notes
- **Listen** — Have the item title and description read aloud
- **Ask AI** — Open a side panel to have AI answer questions about that specific item
- **Save to** — Save the item's answer or your notes to your brain dump or reflections

### Documents & AI Reading
If you upload a PDF or Word doc:
1. The app automatically extracts and reads the text
2. Ask the AI about the document's content — it has read the full document
3. Your questions and answers are saved in a discussion thread you can revisit

## 6. The Pond Rituals

The four orbs in The Pond are your daily practice tools.

### Intention
Set your intention for the day.

**How to use:**
1. Tap the Intention orb (pink lotus)
2. See prompts to reflect on your intention
3. You can write, record, or ask the AI for guidance
4. Your intention is saved and can be reviewed

### Breathwork
Guided breathing exercises for nervous system regulation.

**How to use:**
1. Tap the Breathwork orb
2. Choose a breathing pattern (box breath, 4-7-8, etc.)
3. Follow the on-screen animation
4. The app guides you through cycles

### Focus (Sound & Timer)
Create a focused work session with ambient sound.

**How to use:**
1. Tap the Focus orb
2. Choose your sound (rain, ocean, forest, stream, binaural beats)
3. Set a timer (5 min, 15 min, 30 min, 60 min+)
4. Start — the app plays your sound and tracks time
5. When done, you can save how focused you felt

### Calendar
A mini calendar to see what you've done on each day.

**How to use:**
1. Tap the Calendar orb
2. See a calendar overview of your activities
3. Tap a date to see what you saved or did that day

---

## 7. Know Yourself (AI Hub)

The AI Hub is your companion for exploring ideas, asking questions, and discovering insights.

### Main Features:

**Ask AI**
- Chat with an AI about any of your saved content
- Ask follow-up questions, get summaries, brainstorm ideas
- Each conversation is saved automatically

**Topics to Explore**
- Browse pre-built topics like "Productivity", "Wellness", "Creativity"
- Chat with AI about each topic, with your saved content as context

**Discussions**
- See all your past conversations with AI (organized by haven or item)
- Revisit and continue any discussion
- Export or share conversations

---

## 8. Search Across Everything

Can't find something?

**To search:**
1. Tap the Search icon (magnifying glass) in the top nav
2. Type keywords — search works across:
   - Item titles & descriptions
   - Your notes on items
   - Reflection text
   - Brain dump thoughts
3. Results show up in real-time
4. Tap any result to jump to it

---

## 9. Calm Mode

When you need a break from notifications and distractions.

**To enable Calm Mode:**
1. Tap the "Calm" button in the top nav
2. All animations, floating particles, and the koi fish leap pause
3. The app becomes static and peaceful
4. Tap again to exit Calm Mode

---

## 10. Mobile Experience

Cur8 works on all devices — phone, tablet, desktop.

### Mobile Differences:
- Havens use a tab switcher: Videos | Preview | Docs
- Tap a tab to switch between views
- Items open in a focused preview that fills the screen
- Brain dump and reflections stack vertically for easy typing

### Install as a PWA (Home Screen App)
Make Cur8 feel like a native app on your phone.

**How to install:**
1. On your phone, open Cur8 in your browser
2. Tap the browser menu → "Install" or "Add to home screen"
3. Cur8 appears on your home screen like any other app
4. Tap to open Cur8 without going through the browser

---

## 11. Settings & Account

Access your settings by tapping your profile icon or the menu.

**Available settings:**
- Change your email or password
- Customize where Brain Dump thoughts are sent (default: your email)
- View and manage connected accounts
- Sign out

---

## 12. Tips & Tricks

**Make the most of Cur8:**

1. **Use Brain Dump daily** — It takes 10 seconds and captures fleeting ideas before they disappear
2. **Tag and organise** — Create folders around projects or themes for quick retrieval
3. **Ask the AI** — The AI has read your saved documents and understands your content. Use it to extract insights
4. **Revisit your Reflections** — Patterns emerge when you review past reflections
5. **Use Focus mode with sound** — The ambient sounds + timer combo is powerful for deep work
6. **Try different sorts** — Sometimes sorting by "Recently opened" shows you what's actually important
7. **Calm mode is your friend** — When overwhelmed, tap Calm and just browse your content peacefully
