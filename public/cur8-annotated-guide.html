🌿 Cur8 Annotated Tutorial

Click-by-click walkthrough of every feature

1. Sign In
📱 Welcome Screen
Email Input
Tap the email field and enter your account email address.
Password Input
Tap the password field and enter your password.
Enter My Haven Button
Click the gold button labeled Enter my haven to sign in.
✓ Create Account Link ? Forgot Password
2. Home Screen (Your Haven)
🏠 Main Dashboard
Top Navigation Bar
Left: Cur8 logo · Right: Search · Calm · Guide · Menu
Hero Image (Large Banner)
Beautiful background photo with water ripples and nature elements. Contains greeting "Good morning, [Name]" and your stats below.
Stats Row
Shows: 282 saved · 0 today · 8 havens · Surprise me button
💡 The Surprise Me button shows a random item from your collection — great for rediscovering forgotten saves.
📍 Key Sections (Collapsible)
Know Yourself
Tap to expand → Shows AI chat tabs for exploring topics and reviewing past discussions.
The Pond (Your Daily Rituals)
Shows 4 interactive orbs: Intention (pink lotus) · Breathwork (wind) · Focus (timer) · Calendar
Recently Visited
Horizontal scrollable row of your 8 most recently opened items. Tap any thumbnail to open it.
Your Havens (8 Gardens)
Tap any garden tile to open that haven and see all your content there.
3. Quick Save (Floating Button)
➕ The Gold "+" Button
Location
Bottom-right corner of the screen. Floats above content and stays visible when scrolling.
💾 Save Dialog (Step-by-Step)
1
Tap the + button
The save dialog opens with input options.
2
Paste a link OR upload a file
URL (YouTube, TikTok, articles) or file (PDF, image, voice note)
3
Wait for thumbnail to load
App fetches title, description, and preview image automatically.
4
Choose a haven (garden)
Tap one of the 8 haven tiles or use the dropdown.
5
Add optional notes or attachments
Write notes, attach voice/images. Then tap Paste link from clipboard or similar.
6
Tap Save
Item is saved to your chosen haven. You're done!
💡 Tip:
On mobile, you can also use the PWA share feature — when you tap "Share" in your browser, you can send content directly to Cur8 and it auto-fills the save dialog.
4. Brain Dump (Capture Thoughts)
🧠 Brain Dump Button
Location
Floating pill at bottom-left of the screen. Shows a brain icon and text "Brain Dump".
💬 Brain Dump Dialog (How to Use)
1
Tap Brain Dump button
A modal dialog opens with a text area for your thought.
2
Type your thought OR tap Speak (mic icon)
Either write it out or dictate your idea. The app transcribes speech to text.
3
Optional: Tap Attach to add files
Add voice notes, images, or links to your brain dump.
4
Optional: Tap Tidy (AI feature)
AI analyzes your thought and extracts key points and action items automatically.
5
Tap Save thought (gold button on right)
Your thought is saved and sent to your configured destination (email, WhatsApp, etc.).
Where Thoughts Go
At the bottom of the dialog, you'll see "Where thoughts are sent". This shows your configured destination (usually your personal email). You can customize this in Settings.
5. Opening a Haven (3-Panel Layout)
🏞️ Haven Page Overview
Left Panel: Item List
Shows all your saved items as thumbnails (videos, images, PDFs). Tap an item to preview it. Scroll down to see more.
Centre Panel: Preview
When you tap an item, it shows here. Videos play inline, images display large, PDFs show pages, links show descriptions. Also called the "media focus" area.
Right Panel: Documents & Links
List of all linked websites and documents in this haven. Tap to preview in the centre panel.
🛠️ Haven Toolbar (Top Row)
🧠 Brain Dump ✍️ Reflect 🎵 Focus Sounds
Sort & Filter Row (Below Stats)
Sort (dropdown) · Type Filters (Videos/Images/Documents/Sounds pills) · Folder (shows "FOLDERS" label with folder chip)
📁 Folder Bar (Below Stats, Horizontal Scroll)
Folder Chips
Each folder is a chip you can tap. Shows folder name and item count. Example: "2024 Research (12)" or "Inspiration (8)".
New Folder Button
Click + New folder to create a new folder in this haven.
Sort Pill
Tap Sort to choose sort order (Newest, Oldest, A–Z, Recently opened, By type). Shows currently active sort in gold.
Select Button
Tap Select to enter multi-select mode. Then tap items to check them, and use the batch bar at the bottom to move, copy, delete, or share multiple items at once.
6. Item Details & Actions
📋 Preview Details Bar
Edit Button
Tap to edit the item's title or your personal notes on the item. Changes save automatically.
Listen Button
Tap to hear the title and description read aloud. Useful for accessibility or just listening while doing something else.
Ask AI Button (Message Icon)
Opens a side panel where you can ask the AI questions about this specific item. The AI has read the full document/content and will answer based on actual content, not just the title.
Save / More Options
Tap the three dots or gold button to see options: Copy link, Share (email/WhatsApp), Download, or Save an answer to your brain dump/reflections.
7. The Pond Rituals (4 Orbs)
🎯 Intention Orb (Pink Lotus)
1
Tap the pink lotus orb on the home screen
2
A widget opens with prompt like "What's your intention for today?"
3
Write, record, or ask the AI. Tap Save
🌬️ Breathwork Orb
How to Use
Tap the wind icon orb → choose a breathing pattern (box breath, 4-7-8, etc.) → watch the on-screen animation and follow along. Your breath syncs with the visual guide.
⏱️ Focus Orb (Timer)
How to Use
Tap the timer icon → choose ambient sound (rain, ocean, forest, binaural) → set duration (5/15/30/60 min) → Start. When time is up, you can rate how focused you felt.
📅 Calendar Orb
How to Use
Tap the calendar icon → see a visual calendar → tap a date to see what you saved/did that day. Great for tracking your activities and rituals over time.
8. Search Everything
🔍 Search Feature
1
Tap the Search icon in the top navigation
2
Start typing your search query
Search works across item titles, descriptions, your notes, reflections, and brain dumps.
3
Results appear in real-time below the search box
Results are grouped: Items · Reflections · Brain Dumps
4
Tap any result to jump to it
9. Calm Mode
🧘 Enable Calm Mode
Location
Top navigation bar, next to Search. Button labeled Calm
What It Does
Pauses all animations, removes floating particles, stops the koi fish leap animation, and makes the interface static and peaceful. Perfect for when you just want to browse without distraction.
Exit Calm Mode
Tap the Calm button again to re-enable animations.
10. Install as Mobile App (PWA)
📱 Add to Home Screen (Android)
1
Open Cur8 in Chrome or your phone's browser
2
Tap the browser menu (three dots) → Install app or Add to home screen
3
Tap Install
Cur8 appears on your home screen like any other app. Tap to launch without going through the browser.
💡 Tip:
Once installed as a PWA, you can also use the browser's share feature. When you tap "Share" on a webpage, Cur8 appears as a share target — you can send content directly to Cur8 from anywhere on the web!

🎉 You're ready to use Cur8!

For more help, tap the Guide button in the app's top navigation.
