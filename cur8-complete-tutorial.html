Cur8 Tutorial Guide

Complete walkthrough with screenshots and script

1 Sign In

Start by entering your email and password to access your haven.

Steps:
  1. Enter your email address
  2. Enter your password
  3. Tap the gold "Enter my haven" button
What to say: "Start by opening Cur8 on your phone or desktop. Enter your email and password, then tap 'Enter my haven'. Your session stays active, so once you're in, you stay in."

2 Your Home Screen

Welcome to your personalized dashboard showing your stats and all 8 gardens.

What you see:
  • 282 saved — total items across all havens
  • 0 today — how many you've saved today
  • 8 havens — your content gardens
  • Haven cards below to explore each space
What to say: "Once you're signed in, you land here — your personalized home screen. At the top, you see your stats. Below that are your 8 havens — think of them like folders or rooms in a house. Tap any to explore it."
Tip: The 8 havens are: The Koi Pond (learning), The Current (work), Greenhouse (fashion), Sanctuary (wellness), The Grove (psychology), Ember (inspiration), Bloom (entertainment), The Tide (documents).

3 Inside a Haven

The 3-panel layout: browse items on the left, preview in the center, view details on the right.

The three panels:
  • Left: Thumbnail grid of your saved items
  • Center: Full preview (videos play inline, PDFs page-by-page)
  • Right: Item details and your saved notes
Toolbar buttons (top):
  • Brain Dump — capture fleeting thoughts
  • Reflect (Ask AI) — chat about your saves
  • Focus Sounds — ambient sounds for focus
What to say: "Inside a haven, you're looking at a three-panel view. On the left are your items. In the center is the full preview. On the right are details. Tap any item to preview it. The app fetches the title, thumbnail, and a smart summary."

4 Saving Content

Multiple ways to save: paste links, upload files, or share from other apps.

How to save:
  1. Tap the floating "Save" button
  2. Paste a YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, or article link
  3. Add your own notes (optional but recommended)
  4. Choose which haven to save to
  5. Organize into a folder (optional)
  6. Tap Save — done!
What to say: "Now let's save something new. There's a floating 'Save' button. Tap it, paste a link from YouTube or TikTok, add your notes, choose which haven, and save. The app automatically grabs the title and thumbnail."
Pro tip: On mobile, you can also share directly from YouTube/TikTok/Instagram — Cur8 appears as a share option.

5 Brain Dump - Capture Thoughts

Capture fleeting ideas before they float away. Type, dictate, tidy with AI, and save.

Steps:
  1. Tap the floating Brain Dump pill (bottom-left on mobile)
  2. Type your thought OR tap the mic to dictate
  3. Attach files or saved items (optional)
  4. Tap "Tidy" to let AI clean it up (optional)
  5. Tap "Save thought" and choose where to save it
What to say: "One of my favorite features is Brain Dump. This is where you capture fleeting thoughts — ideas you don't want to lose. You can type, or tap the mic and speak. Then you can let AI 'tidy' it, and save it locally, email, or to mem.ai."
Save options: Local save | Email to yourself | Send to WhatsApp | Send to mem.ai (your external memory).

6 Ask AI About Your Saves

Chat with AI about the content you've saved. It has access to your document text and summaries.

How to use:
  1. While in a haven, tap the "Ask AI" button (speech bubble icon)
  2. Type a question like "Summarize this" or "What are the key points?"
  3. Read the AI response — it's personalized to your actual content
  4. Copy, save to brain dump, or save as a reflection
What to say: "While browsing a haven, tap the 'Ask AI' button. You can ask questions about your saves. The AI has access to your document text, so you get detailed, personalized answers. Every answer can be saved to your brain dump."

7 Focus Sounds - Create Rituals

Ambient sounds to help you focus and build work rituals.

Available sounds:
  • Rain
  • Ocean
  • Forest
  • Stream
  • Binaural 18Hz (focus)
  • Binaural 40Hz (clarity)
How to use:
  1. Tap Focus Sounds button in the toolbar
  2. Choose your sound
  3. Let it loop while you work
What to say: "Cur8 has ambient sounds — rain, ocean, forest, stream, or binaural beats. Choose one, let it loop, and focus on your work. Your brain starts associating that sound with deep work."

8 Mobile View - Tabs

On mobile, the 3-panel layout adapts to tabs so you can swipe between views.

Mobile tabs:
  • Videos tab — browse item thumbnails
  • Preview tab — full preview of selected item
  • Docs tab — item details and notes
What to say: "On mobile, you swipe between tabs at the bottom. Videos, Preview, Docs. Everything works the same, just adapted for the smaller screen."
Tip: Swipe left/right or tap the tabs to switch between views.

9 Search & Sort

Find what you need and organize the view to suit your workflow.

Search:
  • Tap the search bar at the top
  • Type what you're looking for
  • Searches titles, descriptions, and content
Sort options:
  • Newest saved
  • Oldest saved
  • A–Z (alphabetical)
  • Z–A (reverse alphabetical)
  • Recently opened
  • By type (videos, images, docs, sounds)
What to say: "Can't remember where you saved something? Use the search bar. Or tap the Sort button to arrange by newest, oldest, A–Z, or by type."

10 Calm Mode & Discover

Two special modes: Calm for focus, Surprise Me for serendipity.

Calm Mode:
  • Disables all animations
  • Hides notifications
  • Distraction-free view
  • Perfect when overwhelmed
Surprise Me:
  • Tap the gold button on home screen
  • Get a random saved item
  • Great for rediscovering forgotten saves
What to say: "Cur8 has two special features. Calm mode disables animations and notifications. Perfect for focus. And 'Surprise Me' gives you a random item to explore."
